# Expanded Content Balance Notes

## Plants

- `Mist Sprout`: 75 sun, 90 health, slow projectile. Role: lane control with low damage.
- `Thorn Pod`: 100 sun, 95 health, piercing projectile. Role: handles clustered enemies.
- `Bloom Battery`: 75 sun, 85 health, produces 50 sun every 9 seconds. Role: slower economy ramp with stronger payout.
- `Bark Bastion`: 125 sun, 540 health. Role: late defensive wall.
- `Spore Mine`: 50 sun, 45 health, triggers in a short radius and deals 110 area damage. Role: emergency burst.

Existing prototype plants remain valid baseline content:

- `Sprout Blaster`: standard shooter.
- `Sunbud`: cheap economy plant.
- `Stoneleaf`: baseline blocker.

## Enemies

- `Moss Skitter`: 55 health, 0.72 speed, low attack. Role: early pressure.
- `Shellback Shambler`: 150 health, 0.32 speed, 8 armor. Role: punishes low-damage spam.
- `Bloom Carrier`: 115 health, 0.38 speed, extra death reward. Role: target priority and economy swing.
- `Rot Howler`: 260 health, 0.28 speed, lane speed aura. Role: small boss.

Existing prototype enemies remain in the expanded wave:

- `Moss Walker`: baseline enemy.
- `Moss Brute`: heavy baseline enemy.

## Augments

- `First Light`: initial sun +50.
- `Rapid Germination`: plant cooldown -15%.
- `Dense Bark`: plant max health +20%.
- `Sharp Seeds`: projectile damage +15%.
- `Golden Drip`: natural sun interval -20%.
- `Frugal Roots`: plant cost -10%, with at least 5 sun saved.
- `Focused Rows`: shooter attack interval -10%.
- `Prepared Field`: spawns two starting sun pickups.
- `Last Stand`: first defeat trigger is delayed by 3 seconds.
- `Bounty Moss`: enemy death sun rewards +5.

## First Tuning Targets

- Expanded wave should stay under 90 seconds.
- At least one simple strategy should win without relying on a specific augment.
- No single augment should be mandatory for victory.
- `Spore Mine` should solve a local emergency but not replace sustained shooters.
- `Shellback Shambler` should make `Thorn Pod` and stronger projectile damage feel useful without invalidating `Mist Sprout`.
